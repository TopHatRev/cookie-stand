Our Mission

We at Salmon Cookies recognise the disparity between human and Salmon, for centuries, humans have been consuming Salmon for survival and for pleasure. We're here to change this, Salmon Cookies Ltd. have been mastering the art of salmon shaped cookies for over a decade, without including either Salmon or humans in the ingredients, a feat we are personally impressed with.
